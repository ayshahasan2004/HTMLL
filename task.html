Birzeit University

Birzeit University (BZU) is a leading Palestinian institution known for its academic excellence, research, and commitment to social and political engagement. Located near Ramallah, it offers diverse programs in engineering, sciences, humanities, and business. BZU plays a crucial role in fostering critical thinking and innovation in Palestine.
